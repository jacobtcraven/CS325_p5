Super Bowl hype will only grow, and the actual game will only shrink - Yahoo Sports

 The Super Bowl, a spectacle of glitz and hype, drew record viewership. It has transcended football to become a 
 cultural event. Sponsorship costs are exorbitant, and the week leading up to the game is a nonstop hype fest. 
 The game itself provided excitement, but the extravaganza overshadowed it.
