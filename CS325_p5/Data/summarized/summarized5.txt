Spencer Dinwiddie's Lakers contract includes massive $1 bonus for NBA title win - Yahoo Sports

 Spencer Dinwiddie signed with the Lakers for their winning reputation. If they win the championship, he gets a $1 
 bonus, a humorous nod stemming from a previous contract with the Wizards. After bouncing between teams, Dinwiddie joins the 
 Lakers for the 2023-24 season for $1.55 million.
